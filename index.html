Overview

Nexocoin (NXC) is a sovereign, independent digital currency operating on its own native blockchain. It is not a token issued on another network and does not rely on external platforms for consensus, validation, or monetary issuance.

Nexocoin is engineered as a base-layer monetary system with deterministic behavior, transparent on-chain rules, and long-term sustainability as core principles.

Technology

Nexocoin implements all critical components directly at the protocol level: consensus, transaction validation, monetary issuance, and peer-to-peer networking.

The network avoids external dependencies, ensuring robustness, auditability, and true sovereignty.
