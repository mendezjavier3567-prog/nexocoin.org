Nexocoin

Nexocoin (NXC) is a sovereign, independent cryptocurrency operating on its own native blockchain. It is not a token issued on another network and does not depend on external platforms for consensus, validation, or settlement.

The Nexocoin network is designed as a base-layer digital currency system, implementing all core components directly at the protocol level, including consensus, monetary issuance, transaction validation, and network security.

Nexocoin prioritizes security, decentralization, and long-term economic sustainability. The protocol is engineered to provide predictable transaction costs, deterministic behavior, and transparent on-chain rules suitable for real-world usage.

By operating as a fully independent blockchain, Nexocoin avoids inherited risks from external platforms and defines its own security assumptions, performance characteristics, and economic parameters.
