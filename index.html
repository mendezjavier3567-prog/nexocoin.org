Overview

Nexocoin (NXC) is a sovereign digital currency operating on its own native blockchain. It is not a token and does not depend on external platforms for consensus, validation, or monetary issuance.

The protocol is engineered for long-term stability, deterministic behavior, and full on-chain transparency.

Technology

Nexocoin implements all core components directly at the protocol level, including consensus, transaction validation, and monetary policy.

The architecture is designed to be auditable, predictable, and resilient, prioritizing decentralization and security over complexity.
