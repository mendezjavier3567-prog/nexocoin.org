Overview

Nexocoin (NXC) is a sovereign, independent digital currency operating on its own native blockchain. It is not a token, not issued on any external network, and not dependent on third-party platforms.

The protocol is designed as a base-layer monetary system focused on long-term sustainability, cryptographic security, decentralization, and deterministic behavior.

Technology

All core components are implemented directly at the protocol level: consensus, transaction validation, monetary issuance, and governance rules.

Nexocoin minimizes external dependencies, ensuring predictable execution and full on-chain transparency.
