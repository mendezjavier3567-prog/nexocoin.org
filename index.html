Overview

Nexocoin (NXC) is a sovereign, independent digital currency operating on its own native blockchain. It is not issued as a token on any external platform and does not rely on third-party networks for consensus, validation, or monetary issuance.

The Nexocoin protocol is engineered as a base-layer financial system with deterministic execution, transparent on-chain rules, and long-term economic sustainability.

Technology

Nexocoin integrates consensus, transaction validation, and monetary policy directly at the protocol level. All critical components are native to the network.

The architecture prioritizes decentralization, predictable behavior, and resilience against external dependencies, ensuring long-term robustness and auditability.
