Overview

Nexocoin (NXC) is a sovereign, independent digital currency operating on its own native blockchain. It is not a token issued on another network and does not depend on external platforms for consensus, validation, or monetary issuance.

The protocol is engineered as a base-layer digital currency prioritizing decentralization, cryptographic security, deterministic behavior, and long-term sustainability.

Technology

Nexocoin implements all critical components directly at the protocol level, including consensus, transaction validation, monetary supply, and network governance.

This design eliminates external dependencies and ensures transparent, auditable, and predictable on-chain behavior suitable for real-world monetary usage.
